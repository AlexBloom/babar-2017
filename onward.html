Vision for the Future

Overview of current state of Strategic Plan

The operative word for Bike & Build’s future is “diversity.” As we move forward and begin enacting our new strategic plan, we are looking for ways to diversify how we are accomplishing our mission. While we have deep-rooted traditions from the past 15 years that provide a foundation for success, we are excited to explore ways to expand our approach and cultivating organizational growth. We hope to benefit the affordable housing cause in new ways while continuing to empower young adults and foster a generation committed to civic engagement.

Although the strategic plan is still in the works, it is likely to incorporate innovative programming formats; a commitment to cycling safety advocacy; protecting the organization’s financial sustainability; generating new and long-term partnerships; and expanding our rider pool to better reflect the diverse population of America.

Bike & Build has impacted the lives of over 3,500 alumni while making significant contributions to benefit the affordable housing landscape nationwide. We are excited about the future as we look to expand our opportunities and influence at every level.
